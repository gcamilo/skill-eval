# Human Calibration Template

Score each output on the 5 criteria (1-10). Compare your scores to the automated judge to calibrate.

## Scoring Rubric

| Score | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| 1-2   | Missing or fundamentally broken |
| 3-4   | Present but generic, vague, or wrong |
| 5-6   | Acceptable — covers basics, some gaps |
| 7-8   | Good — specific, structured, actionable |
| 9-10  | Excellent — MBB-quality, no gaps |

### Criteria Definitions

- **Structure**: Is the analysis MECE? Clear logic flow from problem to recommendation? Well-organized with headings/hierarchy?
- **Evidence**: Are claims labeled (fact/inference/assumption)? Numbers have units, time periods, sources? No fabricated data?
- **Actionability**: Are recommendations specific (who, what, by when)? Phased? Implementable? Not generic platitudes?
- **Framework Fit**: Did it pick the right framework for this problem type? Avoid framework salad? Apply (not just name) the framework?
- **Completeness**: Has bottom line up front? Risks identified? Next actions listed? Counter-argument addressed?
